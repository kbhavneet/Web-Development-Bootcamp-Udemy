The Best Movies According to Me.

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this crime epic is praised for its storytelling, performances, and intricate portrayal of the Italian-American mafia.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Directed by Frank Darabont and based on a novella by Stephen King, this film is celebrated for its powerful narrative about hope, friendship, and redemption in the face of adversity.

Citizen Kane (1941)

Directed by and starring Orson Welles, this groundbreaking film is renowned for its innovative cinematography, complex narrative structure, and deep exploration of power, ambition, and the human psyche.
